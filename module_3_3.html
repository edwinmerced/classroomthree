Learn


Financial Services. This module will provide a review of the structure and regulatory framework of the US financial institutions, the issues inherent in the evaluation of the liquidity adequacy of financial institutions and the institution’s earning analysis
Learning Targets. Upon the completion of this module you will be able to:

  • Obtain a basic understanding of the structure and regulatory framework of the bank’s financial system
  • Understand the major guidelines for a financial institution performance from a regulator’s perspective
  • Analyze the major components of the financial standing of a financial institution: capital, asset quality, management, earnings and liquidity
  • Obtain a basic understanding of how to assess bank’s management, including the examination of management’s long term strategies and the adequacy of the financial information provided.
  • Assess the level and extent of risk in the financial institution: Interest rate, liquidity and credit risk
  • Examining the new generation financial services sector, its products and services
  • Study the emerging trends in banking technology
