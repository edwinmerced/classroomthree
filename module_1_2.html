Module 1.2: Income Statement Analysis

What is an income statement? What is revenue analysis? What are basic valuation modules? What is a cost of goods sold assessment? What is an operating expense analysis? What is a net profit analysis?

Learn


Income Statement Analysis. In this module we will provide an in-depth review on the analysis of the income statement. The review will be divided in four (4) main sections: a) revenue analysis, b) cost of goods sold assessment, c) operating expense analysis and d) net profit analysis.
Learning Targets. Upon the completion of this module you will be able to:

  • Describe the basic components of the income statement
  • Learn the concept of common sizing financial statements
  • Identify factors that impact revenue analysis
  • Differentiate between expenses associated to cost of goods sold, operating expenses and other income and expense items that impact financial analysis
  • Explain the concepts of trend, break-even and comparative analysis
