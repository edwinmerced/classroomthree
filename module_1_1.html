Module 1.1: Introduction to Financial and Credit Analysis

Learn


Foundations of Financial Management. In this module we will introduce financial statement analysis as an integral part of a broader framework of business analysis. The content includes an examination of the role of financial statement analysis in different business types of analysis such as equity and credit analysis. The module emphasizes the understanding of business activities: planning, financing, investing and operating.
Learning Targets. Upon the completion of this module you will be able to:

  • Explain business analysis and the importance in financial statement analysis
  • Identify and discuss different types of business analysis
  • Identify relevant analysis information beyond the financial statement analysis
  • Define basic valuation modules
  • Define the Value Chain Analysis
