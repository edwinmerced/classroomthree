Module 2.1: The Lending Process

What are the steps of the lending process? What are borrowing structures? What documentation is required for borrowing? What is collateral valuation?

Learn


The Lending Process. This module presents the fundamentals of the lending decision. It is designed to enable you to acquire the skills needed to evaluate business loan applications and to correctly document loans when they have been approved. It is intended to give you a foundation of important concepts and processes on both the financial and human relations skills essential to commercial lending.
Learning Targets. Upon the completion of this module you will be able to:

  • Explain the importance of the lending process
  • Describe the steps in the lending process
  • Identify borrowing structures and describe their advantages
  • Identify most important documentation required to evidence authority to borrow, indebtedness, collateral and loan conditions
  • Collateral valuation and factors associated to liquidity, marketability and control
