Financial Ratios Analysis. The financial analysis process would not be complete without a thorough understanding of financial ratios and the impact in the lending decision. The interpretation of financial statements is often difficult when it is performed in absolute terms. Ratios are comparisons of numbers in the financial statements that assist in placing absolute numbers in context. Through trend and comparative analysis of ratios, a commercial lender can evaluate a companyâ€™s performance in liquidity, financial leverage, coverage, and profitability.
Learning Targets. Upon the completion of this module you will be able to:

  • Calculate and explain the meaning of liquidity, financial leverage, coverage and operating ratios.
  • Apply operating ratios to financial statement analysis
  • Use ratios for industry comparison and trend analysis
